Bean and Cheese Burrito

Burritos for Gamers

Bean and cheese burritos are so damn good. They can satisfy any gamer in a hurry. They only take a few minutes to make and they get better every time you make them.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Toast tortilla over flame of the stove top.
  2. Open beans and spread on tortilla.
  3. Sprinkle cheese on beans.
  4. Douse in sauce of your choice.
  5. Microwave for 1-2 minutes.
