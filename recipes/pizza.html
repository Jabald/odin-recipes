Pizza Time

Pizza is so good, there is a massive variety of pizzas that you will never get sick of it.

I like the classics like Cheese and Pepperoni. But I also like to venture into the unknown and order a BBQ chicken pizza or sometimes a pizza with the works. The works includes a heaping helping of pepperoni, julienne-cut Canadian bacon, spicy Italian sausage, fresh-cut onions, crisp green peppers, mushrooms, ripe black olives, and real cheese made from mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • Cell phone

Steps

  1. Call the Papa over at Papa Johns.
  2. Order your pizza of choice.
  3. Wait 35 minutes for pizza to arrive at the door.
